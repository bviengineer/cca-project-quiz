    Name and describe 4 different kinds of positioning in CSS.

    Four kinds of positioning in CSS are:

    • Static
      • had float right
    • Relative
      • had float left
    • Absolute
    • Fixed

    You can position me anywhere on the page by telling me how far from the left and top you will like for me to be. Call me aboslute positioning.
    Unlike my counterparts, I remain fixed or in place when scrolling and my position is relative to the browser window. Use the top, bottom, left or right properties to position me.
    Hi, I'm a generic <div></div> and I'm in a static position. This is the default position of all elements and as my name implies, I remain in place or within the normal flow of the page.
    I'm also a generic <div></div> but my position is relative. What that means is that though I physically move out of position on the page,the space that I left is held and no other element takes it's place. When I relatively position myself, it's based on my original location or my space in the line. Set my top and left margins in order to move me around.
